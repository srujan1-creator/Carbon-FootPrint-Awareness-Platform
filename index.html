Carbon Literacy Hub

Test your environmental knowledge and browse educational facts about climate change.

🔋

Home Energy Vampires

Up to 10% of household energy is drained by gadgets plugged in but not active (such as phone chargers, televisions, micro-waves). Unplugging them or switching off supply bars saves significant carbon.

🚗

Carbon Cost of Flying

A single return flight from London to New York (approx. 1,600 kg CO₂e) produces more emissions than an average citizen of some developing nations generates in a whole year. Restricting flights has a huge impact.

🐄

Ruminants and Methane

Ruminant livestock (like beef cows and sheep) generate enteric methane emissions as they digest food. Methane is highly warming. Transitioning from beef to chicken cuts emissions by up to 90% per serving.
